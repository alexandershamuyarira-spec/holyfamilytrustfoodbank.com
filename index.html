Our story

We currently work with local Catholic Parishes and support from Society of Saint Vincent de Paul (SSVP) volunteers. It was initially set up in response to Pope Francis's call to feed the elderly during COVID-19 Pandemic lockdown and has continued to serve the elderly and vulnerable people in places like Harare and Chitungwiza;Zimbabwe

Dignity in every delivery

We work with elders and families to ensure food arrives respectfully, safely, and without stigma.

One parcel, many outcomes

Parcel Delivery is done by volunteers who know their Parishes

Each food parcel includes balanced staples, fresh produce, and a message of hope from our volunteer teams.
